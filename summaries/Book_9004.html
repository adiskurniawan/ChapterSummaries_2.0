BACKGROUND
The Foundation is a legally registered foundation in Indonesia that comprises multiple research and support groups and operates at national and field sites. As a recipient of donor funding the Foundation must comply with Indonesian regulations governing foundations and donor accountability and is required to undergo an independent annual financial audit. The objective is to enable the appointed auditor to express an independent opinion on whether the Foundation’s financial statements present fairly in all material respects the financial position results of operations and cash flows for the audit period in accordance with applicable Indonesian financial accounting standards for non-profit entities including SAK-EP and ISAK-35 and relevant tax and ministry of finance regulations.
