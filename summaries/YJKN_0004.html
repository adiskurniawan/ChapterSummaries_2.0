ISAK 335 presentation
Executive recommendation (short): Present performance statements for 1 May 2025 – 31 Dec 2025; statement of financial position as at 31 Dec 2025. Disclose incorporation (February 2025) and commencement of operations (1 May 2025). If auditor requires, present 1 Feb 2025 – 31 Dec 2025 with explicit note that Feb–Apr are nil.
Standards basis (concise): ISAK 335 permits presentation formats tailored for non-profit entities so statements reflect the timing and nature of resource flows; combine ISAK 335 with PSAK/IAS 1 presentation principles (identify reporting period, disclose shortened periods, disclose basis of preparation). Presentation choice affects only presentation, not recognition or measurement.
Why prefer 1 May–31 Dec 2025 (technical logic):
1. Performance statements should show the period when economic events occurred — Feb–Apr had no transactions so no decision-useful information is added.
2. Avoids misleading visual implication of activity during Feb–Apr.
3. Keeps P&L and cash-flow focused and reduces explanatory clutter in tables.
4. Totals (surplus/deficit, equity, cash) are identical under either choice — no measurement or audit risk.
When to choose 1 Feb–31 Dec 2025 instead: regulator/internal policy requires reporting from incorporation; auditor insistence; or corporate/legal reporting templates that must match incorporation date. If used, add an explicit note that transactions commenced 1 May 2025 and Feb–Apr amounts are nil.
Exact statement headings — Option A (recommended):
1. Statement of financial position — As at 31 December 2025
2. Statement of activities — For the period 1 May 2025 to 31 December 2025
3. Statement of changes in equity (net assets) — For the period 1 May 2025 to 31 December 2025
4. Statement of cash flows — For the period 1 May 2025 to 31 December 2025
5. Notes — For the period 1 May 2025 to 31 December 2025 (include basis of preparation and ISAK 335 reference).
Exact statement headings — Option B (auditor preference):
1. Statement of financial position — As at 31 December 2025
2. Statement of activities — For the period 1 February 2025 to 31 December 2025
3. Statement of changes in equity — For the period 1 February 2025 to 31 December 2025
4. Statement of cash flows — For the period 1 February 2025 to 31 December 2025
5. Notes must state: “Transactions commenced 1 May 2025; amounts prior to this date are nil.”
Copy-paste note texts (ready to use):
1. Incorporation & commencement — “The Foundation was incorporated on [DD February 2025]. The Foundation commenced operations and recorded its first transactions on 1 May 2025.”
2. Reporting period (Option A) — “These financial statements cover the period 1 May 2025 to 31 December 2025 and were authorised for issue by the Board on [date]. The statement of financial position is presented as at 31 December 2025.”
3. Reporting period (Option B) — “These financial statements cover the period 1 February 2025 to 31 December 2025. The Foundation recorded no transactions from 1 February 2025 to 30 April 2025; amounts for that period are nil.”
4. Comparative information — “Comparative information for the preceding period is not presented because the Foundation had no transactions prior to the reporting period.”
5. Basis of preparation & ISAK 335 framing — “The financial statements have been prepared in accordance with PSAK/IFRS presentation requirements and with the considerations for non-profit entities described in ISAK 335. Presentation formats have been selected to reflect the timing of the Foundation’s economic activity.”
6. Opening balances / first financial statements — “These are the Foundation’s first financial statements. Opening balances at the opening date represent initial recognition of capital contributions, donor funds and pre-operational payments, as disclosed in Note X.”
Short auditor scripts (verbatim):
1. Collaborative — “We accept that February–December is permissible. Because there were no transactions before 1 May 2025, we propose presenting the statement of activities, statement of changes in net assets, and statement of cash flows for 1 May–31 Dec 2025, with full disclosure of the February incorporation and May commencement in the notes. This does not change any reported amounts. We will adopt February–December if you prefer.”
2. Technical (standards) — “Under ISAK 335 presentation for non-profit entities may be tailored to reflect the timing of resource flows. Combined with PSAK/IAS 1’s requirement to identify the reporting period and disclose shortened periods, presenting 1 May–31 Dec 2025 is consistent with standards and preserves audit assertions. We will attach ISAK 335 excerpts and GL working papers demonstrating nil activity Feb–Apr.”

1. Headings reflect chosen period and are consistent across FS.
2. Incorporation and commencement notes inserted and dated.
