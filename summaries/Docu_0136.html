Practical recommendations (if you plan to donate, partner, or evaluate further)
- Request the latest audited financial statements and program-specific budgets for the project you plan to support. (Essential for due diligence.)

- Confirm geographic coverage to ensure your support reaches the communities you intend to target.
- If funding a new program, structure a small pilot + measurable KPIs, then scale based on independent verification.
