Payroll Budget & Forecast Manual
Executive summary & purpose
This manual defines an operational, audit-ready approach to constructing monthly payroll budgets and multi-period forecasts that incorporate PPh Article 21 withholding (monthly TER-based withholding and year-end Pasal 17 reconciliation), employer and employee BPJS (Health & Manpower) contributions, and the accounting/journal flows and e-Bupot reporting required for compliance.
Deliverables: per-employee monthly budget workbook, consolidated payroll cost forecast, cash-flow sensitivity lines for tax and benefit payments (including NTPN timing), e-Bupot export templates and a standardized audit pack index for each payroll run and year-end.
The manual contains explicit control-variable lists, RACI matrices, preflight validation scripts (pseudocode), exception triage flows, sample journal templates, and an implementation roadmap with go/no-go gates. Regulatory references and effective dates must be captured in the Control_Variables sheet and stored as immutable source links for each payroll cycle.
Scope & intended users
Audience: payroll preparers, payroll reviewers/tax specialists, HR master-data owners, treasury/payments staff, internal/external auditors, IT/integration engineers, and vendor support for e-Bupot.
Coverage: master data governance, monthly forecasting & accrual logic, TER monthly withholding and flags for annualization, Pasal 17 year-end reconciliation, gross-up mechanics, BPJS employer cost modelling (Health & Ketenagakerjaan), e-Bupot import/export & error handling, GL mapping & ERP upload prep, audit-pack assembly, retention and evidencing rules, and automation/integration guidance.
Exclusions: payroll legal advice, employee-specific tax dispute resolution (refer to tax counsel), and third-party system configuration outside standardized templates.

Each variable must store: value, source_url_or_doc, source_effective_date, loaded_by, load_date, change_reason, version_id.
Key authoritative references (must-check before each budget cycle)

For each source record: document title, publication ID, published date, effective date, and attached PDF copy in control repository. Flag any differences from prior version in a Regulatory_Change_Log.
Terminology — precise definitions used in the model
- Gross Pay: sum of all earnings before deductions (salary, allowances, overtime, bonuses, THR, taxable natura).
- Taxable Monthly Base (TER basis): monthly figure used to compute TER after permitted monthly deductions.
- TER: monthly average effective withholding rate used for monthly withholding where allowed; not a replacement for year-end Pasal 17 calculation.
- PKP (Penghasilan Kena Pajak): annual taxable income used with Pasal 17 progressive bands.
- KB / LB: outstanding underpayment (Kurang Bayar) / overpayment (Lebih Bayar) after year-end reconciliation.
- NTPN: tax payment reference receipt.
Control variables (centralized, single source of truth)
Design: single protected sheet or configuration store with field types and validation rules. Minimum fields and types: PTKP_CODE (string), PTKP_ANNUAL (number), PASAL17_BANDS (json array), TER_FLAG (boolean), BPJS_HEALTH_EMP_RATE (decimal), BPJS_HEALTH_EMPLOYEE_RATE (decimal), BPJS_HEALTH_WAGE_CAP (number), BPJS_KET_WAGE_CAPS (dict), EBUPOT_VERSION (string), GROSSUP_POLICY_ID (ref), EXCHANGE_RATE_TABLE (date-keyed table). Governance: sheet locked, change request workflow, timestamped versioning, and checksum. Store regulatory_source (doc id) and effective_date for each variable.

Per-employee monthly calculation flow (detailed)
Step-by-step logic (each with required inputs, validation and audit outputs):
1. Gross calculation: sum fixed salary + variable allowances + overtime + bonuses + taxable natura valuation. Validation: compare to Master_Data baseline and Monthly_Input changes; flag >X% variance.
2. Permitted deductions: pension employee contribution, pensionable caps, authorized pre-tax reductions. Persist deduction codes and amounts per employee.
3. TER taxable base: apply monthly prorating or annualization assumptions per TER rules; store TER_Input_Assumptions for audit.
4. Apply TER: compute monthly PPh withheld using TER algorithm; store TER_rate_used, TER_version, TER_annualized_projection and YTD_withheld_after_month.
5. BPJS deductions: compute employee & employer BPJS components separately; apply component-specific wage base and caps; attach rule ID and source doc.
6. Net pay & payslip: Net = Gross − employee BPJS − employee pension − PPh withheld (unless employer-borne). Record payslip snapshot (redact PII for audit pack).
7. Journal generation: auto-generate accrual lines (Payroll Expense, Accrued Payroll, Accrued PPh21, Accrued BPJS) with narrative templates and PayrollRunID.
TER (Average Effective Rate) implementation — practical notes
Design patterns and control points:
- Algorithm: follow DJP guidance; document annualization assumptions (e.g., expected months of pay, expected one-offs).
- Flags: TER_apply_flag per employee, TER_override_reason if TER not used (probation, new join, large one-off).
- Audit trace: store inputs (monthly gross series), annualized projection, TER rate, per-month withheld, and a TER_reconciliation_score = |Pasal17_projection − TER_projection| / Pasal17_projection. Flag > threshold for manual review.
- Year-end: compute full annual Pasal 17 and generate KB/LB per employee; produce bulk summary and detailed per-employee reconciliations.
Gross-up handling (employer-borne tax) — robust recipe
Process, recordkeeping and sample solver pseudocode:
- Policy: define scope (what income elements included), permissible rounding, and approval levels for gross-up cases.
- Solver: iterative numerical solver (Goal Seek) or closed-form where available. Required stored items: GrossPre, GrossGrossed, Tax_on_Grossed, EmployerCostIncrease, SolverTolerance, SolverVersion.
- Pseudocode (for audit):
function gross_up_target(net_target, deduction_rules):
  guess = net_target
  loop until convergence:
    tax = compute_pas17_or_TER(guess)
    net = guess - employee_deductions - tax
    adjust guess based on (net_target - net)
  return guess, tax
- Evidence: attach solver log, inputs, iteration steps, tolerance and final payslip to the audit pack.
BPJS (Health & Manpower) modelling — breakdown and caps
Detailed component rules and engineering considerations:
- Separate each BPJS component as its own calculation block with: WageBase, Rate, Cap, AppliedWage, ContributionAmount, RoundRule, SourceDoc.
- BPJS Health: employer & employee rates, wage base cap (control variable). Ensure AppliedWage = MIN(EligibleEarnings, BPJS_HEALTH_WAGE_CAP) and store cap_applied_flag.
- BPJS Ketenagakerjaan: implement JHT, JKK, JKM, JP with component-specific wage bases and caps; compute employer-only components separately and show total employer cost per employee.
- Edge cases: multiple employments in same group, part-time pro-rata, expatriate schemes (exemptions), and retroactive pay adjustments.
BPJS & Tax interaction — practical observations
- Define which items are BPJSable (salary, allowances, THR, severance) per policy & circulars; store as boolean flags per earning type.
- Payment timing: payroll run pays net pay; employer BPJS payments often separate due to aggregation — map these to treasury schedule and include payment lags in cash forecast.
- Reversals & adjustments: when payroll retro adjustments occur, the model must support month-of-origin correction vs. current-month adjustment and produce corrective e-Bupot rows if required.

Master Data — scope & purpose

Canonical Master_Data columns — personal data
• FullName — string, source name.
• NameNormal — string, normalized uppercase name.
• ContractType — enum: permanent | contract | temp.
• EmploymentStartDate — date.
• EmploymentEndDate — date | NULL.

Canonical Master_Data columns — banking & payment
• BankName — string.
• BankAccountMasked — string format ****1234.
• BankAccountHash — sha256, mandatory if BankAccountMasked present.
• SalaryCurrency — ISO4217.
• SalaryBase — numeric ≥ 0.

Canonical Master_Data columns — compensation structure
• FixedAllowances — JSON or structured columns.
• VariableAllowances — JSON.
• OvertimeRate — numeric ≥ 0.
• PensionSchemeCode — string.

Canonical Master_Data columns — organization mapping
• CostCenter — string.
• Department — string.

Canonical Master_Data columns — integrity & audit
• MasterDataChecksum — sha256 checksum of the full row snapshot.

Row-level validation rules
• NPWP must match ^\d{15}$ where required.
• PTKP_Code must be present and valid.
• EmploymentEndDate ≥ EmploymentStartDate when not NULL.

Conditional mandatory rules

TER algorithm — pseudocode, assumptions & corner cases
Pseudocode (audit-grade):
function apply_TER(monthly_history, forecast_oneoffs, months_expected, pasal17_bands, ptkp, deductions):
annual_proj = sum(monthly_history) + sum(forecast_oneoffs for remaining months)
annual_deductions = sum(allowed_annual_deductions) + PTKP_Annual(ptkp) + sum(deductions)
pkp = max(0, annual_proj - annual_deductions)
tax_due = apply_pasal17(pkp, pasal17_bands) // progressive brackets logic
ter_rate = tax_due / max(1, annual_proj) // avoid divide-by-zero; flag zero-projection
monthly_withheld = round_per_rule(ter_rate * current_month_base, ROUNDING_RULE)
return {ter_rate, tax_due, annual_proj, monthly_withheld, inputs_hash}
Assumptions: forecast_oneoffs documented and approved; months_expected based on contract and pro-rata rules. Edge rules: new joins within run month default TER_apply_flag = FALSE unless approved; probationary employees may use override tax table; employees with multi-employer income require manual PKP aggregation and external evidence; TER_reconciliation_score = abs(pasal17_projection − ter_projection) / max(1, pasal17_projection); if > TER_RECON_THRESHOLD then create TER_Review ticket. Store all inputs, versions, and intermediate steps for audit.

Ruang Lingkup & Siapa yang Terlibat
Ruang lingkup: seluruh aktivitas pemotongan PPh 21 untuk pegawai tetap, pegawai tidak tetap, penerima pensiun berkala, dan pembayaran terkait pekerjaan/jasa.

Terminologi Kunci (definisi singkat)
• Bupot = Bukti Potong PPh 21 (mis. 1721-A1).
• TER = Tarif Efektif Rata-rata bulanan.
• Masa Pajak Terakhir = Desember atau bulan berhenti bekerja/pensiun.
• PTKP = Penghasilan Tidak Kena Pajak.
• PKP = Penghasilan Kena Pajak.
• Gross-up = Pajak ditanggung pemberi kerja dan digrosskan ke penghasilan bruto.
Landasan Regulasi (ringkas, rujukan internal)
Ikuti peraturan fiskal yang berlaku dan pedoman kantor pusat: UU PPh, PP terkait PPh 21, PMK teknis (mis. PMK-168/PMK.03/2023), dan peraturan DJP tentang bentuk & tata cara pembuatan bukti potong serta e-Bupot.

• PPh 21 diakui sesuai periode terutang (accrual): beban gaji diakui saat jasa diberikan; pajak diakui sebagai liabilitas saat pemotongan (atau pada saat penghitungan tahunan bila masa akhir menghasilkan kewajiban tambahan).

Modul 1 — Regulasi, Terminologi & Kebijakan Internal
Konten:
- Hirarki peraturan (UU PPh, PP, PMK, PER) dan implikasinya pada proses pemotongan;
- Definisi pegawai tetap vs tidak tetap, penerima pensiun, subjek pajak dalam negeri vs luar;
- Definisi masa pajak terakhir;
- Kebijakan internal (apakah perusahaan menyediakan tax benefit/allowance/gross-up).
Narasi: peserta mesti memahami bahwa PERUSAHAAN harus menerapkan aturan di atas secara ketat dan mencatat semua kebijakan tambahan yang mempengaruhi perhitungan (mis. kebijakan gross-up, pembagian tunjangan).
Modul 2 — Master Data & Kontrol Kualitas Data
Konten:
- Field wajib: NPWP (format numeric tanpa pemisah), NIK/paspor, status PTKP awal tahun, tanggal mulai/berhenti, rekening bank (bila pengembalian), alamat, kode negara domisili untuk WNA.
- Kontrol: dua-level approval untuk perubahan NPWP/rekening; log audit; periodic data certification (quarterly).
- Praktik: run data quality report — missing PTKP, duplikat NPWP, mismatch nama vs NPWP.
Narasi: master data yang salah adalah sumber 90% kesalahan pajak; prosedur pencegahan harus dipraktekkan (hard block saat run bila NPWP missing).
Modul 3 — Klasifikasi Penghasilan & Penilaian Natura/Kenikmatan
Konten:
- Komponen penghasilan teratur: gaji pokok, tunjangan tetap, fasilitas rutin.
- Komponen penghasilan tidak teratur: bonus, THR, tantiem, gratifikasi, premi.
- Natura/kenikmatan: penilaian manfaat apartemen, kendaraan, fasilitas sekolah; pembeda antara natura non-objek sesuai ketentuan dan yang kena pajak.
- Praktik: nilai bunga fasilitas, nilai sewa pasar, penentuan taxable benefit.
Narasi: dokumentasikan metodologi penilaian natura; gunakan asumsi yang konsisten (mis. market rent x occupancy proportion).
Modul 4 — Pengurang Penghasilan & PTKP (Praktik)
Konten:
- Biaya jabatan/pensiun (contoh rumus: 5% dari penghasilan bruto setahun, capped per bulan/tahun sesuai jenis pegawai), iuran pensiun yang dibayar pegawai, zakat wajib melalui pemberi kerja.
- Penentuan PTKP berdasarkan status pada 1 Jan tahun berjalan; aturan prorata jika wajib pajak baru di tengah tahun — catat kebijakan perusahaan.
Narasi: pelatihan menekankan audit trail untuk seluruh potongan yang diklaim sebagai pengurang.
Modul 5 — TER Bulanan: Konsep, Perbandingan & Dampak Praktis
Konten:
- Penjelasan TER (Tarif Efektif Rata-rata) dan kapan dipakai: untuk pemotongan tiap masa pajak selain masa terakhir;
- Perbandingan hasil TER vs perhitungan tahunan (ilustrasi beban pajak tahunan sama, pergeseran kas antar bulan);
- Praktik: gunakan tabel TER dan hitung pajak tiap bulan untuk 6 profil pegawai; identifikasi selisih yang muncul di masa akhir.
Narasi: peserta mesti paham bahwa TER mempermudah pemotongan bulanan tetapi tidak menggantikan rekonsiliasi tahunan.
Modul 6 — Penghitungan PPh 21 pada Masa Pajak Terakhir (Langkah-demi-langkah)
Langkah operasional:
1. Kumpulkan seluruh penghasilan bruto setahun (termasuk natura).
2. Hitung total pengurang setahun (biaya jabatan, iuran pensiun, zakat).
3. Dapatkan Penghasilan Neto Setahun = Bruto Setahun − Pengurang.
4. Kurangi PTKP setahun → PKP.
5. Hitung PPh terutang setahun menggunakan tarif progresif berlapis (Pasal 17).
6. Kurangi jumlah PPh yang telah dipotong masa-masa sebelumnya.
7. Hasil = Kurang Bayar (KB) atau Lebih Bayar (LB) pada masa akhir.
Catatan operasional: simpan per-bulan breakdown untuk memudahkan audit.
Modul 7 — Gross-Up (PPh Ditanggung Pemberi Kerja)
Konten:
- Konsep: jika pemberi kerja menanggung PPh, jumlah pajak menjadi benefit yang harus digrosskan sehingga beban pajak tidak mengurangi take-home pay karyawan;
- Rumus iterative sederhana: mencari G sehingga Tax(G) = declared employer tax;
- Langkah praktikal: gunakan iterative solver Excel; aktifkan iterative calculation; validasi hasil dengan sample payslip dan perbandingan net pay sebelum/dengan gross-up.
Narasi: gross-up jadi lebih kompleks untuk komponen naturals & THR; dokumentasikan asumsi dan simulasi.
Modul 8 — e-Bupot 21/26: Impor CSV, Validasi & Posting SPT Masa

Contoh Kasus Panjang — Studi Terpandu (narasi & steps)
Case: Senior Manager menerima gross-up untuk gaji bulan Desember besar, pindah pemberi kerja bulan September, dan menerima bonus pada November.
Langkah-langkah:
1. Kumpulkan seluruh bukti dari pemberi kerja lama (A1) — bila disediakan.
2. Rekap penghasilan Jan–Des, sertakan bonus Nov, gaji Des gross-up calculation & iterative solution.
3. Hitung setahun, bandingkan PPh yang dipotong Jan–Nov (TER) dan PPh setahun; tetapkan KB/LB.
4. Jika LB individu, proses pengembalian: authorise → transfer → bukti disimpan.
5. Posting A1 di e-Bupot, include previous employer A1 jika ada, dan simpan audit pack lengkap.
Catatan: dokumentasikan komunikasi pegawai & bukti serah terima A1 dari pemberi kerja lama.

FAQ Operasional (singkat & praktis)
Q: Apa yang harus dilakukan jika NPWP pegawai tidak ada saat payroll close?
A: Block payment run for that employee; escalate to HR; require NPWP or formal waiver; document decision.

Q: Bagaimana memperlakukan PPh ditanggung pemberi kerja?
A: Lakukan gross-up calculation, treat employer tax as taxable benefit, produce revised payslip and journal entries; retain solver output.

Q: Bagaimana bila ada LB namun pegawai sudah resign?
A: Proses pengembalian ke rekening terakhir; jika tidak tersedia, komunikasikan dengan pegawai untuk metode pembayaran; simpan bukti in audit pack.
