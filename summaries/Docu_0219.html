Q: Why is it important to register and manage BPJS Kesehatan through the official Mobile JKN app?
A: Show answer
Mobile JKN is the official self-service channel for many participant actions, so it reduces dependence on branch visits for routine administration. The official BPJS Kesehatan pages show that the app supports account access, participant administration, service access, iuran information, queueing, complaints, and other membership functions. That matters because a participant’s data, payment status, and service choices are connected. When those records are handled in one official place, the risk of missing information is lower and the process is easier to trace later. The app is also supported by official guides and video tutorials, which makes it easier to teach new users and standardize the process in a family or office setting.

Send to the team:
- “Please use Mobile JKN as the main channel for routine BPJS Kesehatan administration.”
Key point:
- One official app covers many common participant tasks.
- Using the same channel reduces inconsistent data handling.

Q: Why should participants create an official Mobile JKN account instead of relying only on branch-office service?
A: Show answer
An official account gives the participant direct access to self-service features without waiting for office hours or queueing for every minor change. BPJS Kesehatan’s account guide shows login using NIK or No KK, password, and captcha, while the video guide also includes account creation, biometric login, password reset, PIN changes, password changes, saved username, and notification features. That means the account is not just a login screen; it is the starting point for a controlled digital membership record. When the account exists and is used properly, the participant can check details, update contact information, and reach service menus faster. It also makes it easier to restore access when the phone changes or the password is forgotten.

Send to the team:

Key point:
- The account is the gateway to most Mobile JKN services.
- Secure account access helps avoid repeated manual service requests.

Q: Why should a participant use REHAB when there are contribution arrears?
A: Show answer
REHAB is BPJS Kesehatan’s installment payment plan for eligible participants with arrears. The official guide explains that it is intended for PBPU and Bukan Pekerja participants with more than 3 months of arrears, specifically in the 4 to 24 month range. That makes it useful when the participant cannot clear the entire balance at once but still wants a structured way to catch up. The app shows the total arrears for the family, a simulation, terms and conditions, and the OTP step. In practice, REHAB is helpful because it turns a large unpaid amount into a more manageable plan and gives the participant a formal route back to regular status.

Send to the team:
- “Please use REHAB when BPJS Kesehatan arrears need to be paid in stages.”

- Installments can make repayment more manageable.
