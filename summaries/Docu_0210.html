Vacancy
HRIS and Payroll Officer

Full time, onsite role based in Jakarta / Karawang, Indonesia.

We are looking for an HRIS and Payroll Officer to maintain the Human Resources Information System, support Employee Self-Service features, execute payroll accurately and on time, and ensure employee records are secure, confidential, and up to date.

ABOUT THE ROLE
The HRIS and Payroll Officer is responsible for maintaining employee data, supporting HR system processes, processing payroll with accuracy, and coordinating closely with HR and Finance to ensure all employee changes are reflected correctly. The role also prepares headcount and employee data reports for internal use and management needs.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Maintain and develop the HRIS and support Employee Self-Service processes.
- Process payroll accurately and on time while ensuring compliance with labor laws and tax regulations.
- Maintain payroll records and protect the confidentiality and security of employee data.
- Coordinate with HR and Finance on new hires, terminations, promotions, and salary adjustments.
- Generate accurate headcount and employee data reports.

WHAT YOU’LL NEED TO SUCCEED
- Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field.
- Experience in HRIS, payroll, or HR administration.
- Knowledge of payroll and taxation, including PPh 21.
- Experience with HRIS platforms such as Sunfish is an advantage.
- High attention to detail, strong numerical skills, discretion, and integrity.

WHY THIS ROLE MATTERS
This role keeps employee data clean and payroll correct. It is a position for someone who is careful, disciplined, and dependable, and who understands that small errors in HRIS or payroll can affect employees, compliance, and trust.

100. Final interview mindset

Your job is to make it easy for them to imagine you in the role: steady, precise, discreet, and dependable. Keep the answers plain, grounded, and specific. That is what will sound strongest.
