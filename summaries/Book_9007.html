1. Background & Scope Intra-Programme transactions are payments made by one OUCRU unit while expenditure is recognised by another. Legal entities: OUCRU Vietnam (HCM & HN), EOCRU / OUCRU Indonesia, OUCRU Nepal, PAHS. OUCRU Nepal and PAHS are excluded. Scope: procedures apply to OUCRU VN and OUCRU IND.
2. Purpose Approval Codes (AC) are unit/entity specific and not usable cross-geography. Recommend separate ACs and budgets per entity. Exceptions allowed for one-off / unanticipated / low-value transactions where a workaround is needed.
3. Responsibilities HoF VN and HoF IND are responsible for correct recording, monthly settlement and periodic (ideally monthly) reconciliation. Definitions: 1st Unit = payer on behalf; 2nd Unit = expense owner. A unit may act as 1st or 2nd Unit at different times.
4. Procedure — 1st Unit (payer on behalf) 1) Approval: invoice/expense claim follows OUCRU approval process. Due date: ??? Who: in-charge staff.
2) Posting: record only when payment made; post as balance-sheet via Inter-company account (Account 360*) not as expenditure.
3) Share docs/details immediately after posting: original may be retained by 1st Unit; scanned copy + GL extraction (amounts, item date, GBP/USD/EUR equivalents, exchange rates) sent to 2nd Unit.
4) Reconcile monthly with 2nd Unit; escalate discrepancies to CFO.
5. Procedure — 2nd Unit (expense owner) 1) Receive supporting docs and GL details. Due date: ??? Who: in-charge staff.
2) Post when payment made as balance-sheet intercompany entry (see Section 4.2 GL journals).
3) Complete GL entry and notify 1st Unit HoF.
4) Reconcile monthly with 1st Unit; escalate discrepancies to CFO.
6. 4.2 Accounting entries — Case 1 (No cross-legal AC) Context: Invoice paid by OUCRU Vietnam for OUCRU Indonesia — Approval code IND99. Invoice = VND 1,000,000.
1st Unit (OUCRU VN):
Journal 1: Dr 360111 Intercompany – EOCRU 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD → Cr 111*/112* Cash/Bank 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD. [1]
Journal 2: Dr Oxford Advance (AROX) 230 USD / 190 GBP / 230 USD → Cr 821* Currency Control (230 USD / 190 GBP / 230 USD).
Dr 821* 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD → Cr 360111 Intercompany – EOCRU 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD; Cr FX gain/loss 10 GBP / 20 USD.
2nd Unit (OUCRU IND):
Journal 1: Dr 7* Expenses (AC 99IND) 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD → Cr 360311 Intercompany – OUCRU VN 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD. [2]
Journal 2: Dr 360311 Intercompany – OUCRU VN 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD → Cr Grant Income 1,000,000 VND / 200 GBP / 250 USD.
7. 4.2 Accounting entries — Case 2 (Cross-legal AC exists) If cross-legal ACs and split budgets are set up in advance, normal operating procedures apply at each entity and no workaround is required.
