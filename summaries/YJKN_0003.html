AP Remediation Playbook — Support Pack
Purpose & scope:
Centralized operational pack to support the Long-Outstanding AP email series. Provides templates, column orders, stepwise runbooks, controls, escalation paths, reporting KPIs, and audit-retention rules required to execute remediation consistently and auditable. Applies to AP, Treasury, Vendor Relations, Business Units, Legal, Tax, and Finance Managers.
Deliverable templates & filenames (canonical):
• ap_aging_vendor_invoice.csv — columns: canonicalVersion, vendorId, vendorName, invoiceNumber, invoiceDate, dueDate, amount, currency, balance, daysPastDue, departmentCode, PO_Number, holdReason, ownerDept, evidenceRef.
• ap_returned_payments.csv — columns: paymentRef, paymentDate, invoiceNumber, vendorId, amount, bankReturnCode, bankTraceId, returnReason, processedBy, status, retransferEvidenceRef.
• vendor_outreach_log.xlsx — sheet per vendor; columns: contactDate, contactMethod, contactName, responseSummary, responseReceivedTs, nextStep, attachmentRef.
• dispute_packet_<invoiceNumber>.zip — packaged PDF/scan set: PO, GRN, approvals, invoice, correspondence, receipts, internal notes.
Bank trace & treasury fields (minimum):
bankTraceId, originatingBank, originAccountMask, beneficiaryBank, beneficiaryAccountMask, valueDate, amount, currency, returnCode, returnReasonText, swiftAdvicePath.
Vendor outreach templates (copy-ready):
Subject: Vendor Confirmation Required — Invoice [invoiceNumber] — [CompanyName]
Body: Please confirm receipt/status of payment for invoice [invoiceNumber] amount [amount currency]. Bank trace: [bankTraceId]. If funds not received, please provide confirmation or preferred retransfer details within 3 business days. Attach confirmations to the vendor_outreach_log.
Dispute packet checklist (per invoice):
1. Purchase Order and approvals.
2. Goods Received Note or service completion evidence.
3. Original vendor invoice and remittance advices.
4. Email/letter correspondence with vendor.
5. Any credit notes or applied vendor ledger entries.
6. Treasury bank trace / SWIFT advice if payment attempted.
7. Business unit root-cause memo (who, what, why, remediation).
Approval package checklist (for settlement / write-off):
• Summary sheet: invoice list, original amount, proposed action, recommended amount, rationale.
• Supporting dispute packet links.
• Legal memo if exposure > policy threshold.
• Tax memo if potential tax treatment implications.
• CFO sign-off line and Finance Manager endorsement.
Temporary controls & operational rules:
• Freeze rule: suspend new PO approvals for vendors with unresolved >90-day or >1-year flags unless explicit CFO pre-approval recorded.
• Payment release rule: two-authorizer requirement for any payment outside standard terms while remediation is open.
• System flags: set bounceBackFlag, >90Flag, >1YearFlag fields on AP master record and require LegalReviewRequired checkbox for write-offs.
Execution runbook (stepwise):
1. AP: export ap_aging_vendor_invoice.csv and tag ownerDept. (Due T+0, 1 business day).
2. AP+Treasury: reconcile payments, produce ap_returned_payments.csv. (Due T+2 business days).
3. Vendor Relations / AP: run vendor outreach using template and record into vendor_outreach_log.xlsx. (Due T+5 business days).
4. Business units: assemble dispute_packet per invoice and upload. (Due T+5–7 business days).
5. Finance Manager: collate approval packages and submit for CFO/Legal/Tax review. (Due T+10 business days).
6. AP/Treasury: execute approved remedial actions and post journal entries; update files and close items.
Escalation matrix & thresholds:
• Tier 1 — vendor balance >$10k and >90 days: escalate to Finance Manager within 3 business days.
• Tier 2 — balance >$50k or >1 year: auto-escalate to CFO + Legal + Tax and tag for mandatory review.
• Tier 3 — returned payments with no trace after 5 business days: escalate to Treasury Head and Compliance.
KPIs & dashboard fields (for management):
• TotalAgedAP, Aged>30, Aged>60, Aged>90, Aged>365, BounceBackCount, BounceBackAmount, Top10VendorExposure, DaysToResolutionMedian, OpenBlockingCount.
Visual tiles: vendor confirmation parity, count of dispute packets missing docs, top owners by outstanding amount.
Audit, retention & evidentiary rules:
• Keep dispute packets and bank traces for minimum 7 years or as required by local tax law.
• Each remediation event must append an AuditLog entry with: actionType, actorId, timestampUTC, manifestHash of the zipped packet.
• Use WriteAtomicFile pattern for persisting PDFs and evidence bundles to avoid partial states.
Naming conventions & folder taxonomy:
• Shared folder root: /Finance/AP/Remediation/YYYYMMDD/.
• File names: ap_aging_YYYYMMDD.csv, returned_payments_YYYYMMDD.csv, dispute_packet_<invoiceNumber>_YYYYMMDD.zip.
• Tagging: include manifestHash and versionId in file metadata for parity checks.
Communication & handoff rules:
• When assigning ownership, reply-all to the originating email with exact file path and owner column filled.
• If not owner, declare accountable department in reply within 24 hours.
Quick reference snippets (shared README):
• Vendor outreach subject/body templates.
• Column-order CSV header rows for AP exports.
• Escalation contacts list with phone and pager IDs.
Acceptance criteria for close of remediation item:
1. Evidence of vendor confirmation or settled payment.
2. Updated GL and AP aging reflects zero or correctly reclassified balance.
3. Dispute packet archived and AuditLog entry appended with manifestHash.
4. Controls updated if systemic issue detected.
